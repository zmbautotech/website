Our Journey

Founded in 2015 as ZMB Autotech (Enterprise), we began our mission to provide exceptional automotive care with a specialization in European vehicles. Our commitment to excellence and customer satisfaction quickly established us as a trusted name in Seremban.

In 2022, we took a significant step forward by incorporating as ZMB (M) SDN BHD, solidifying our commitment to professional automotive solutions and expanding our capabilities to serve both individual car owners and corporate clients.

Today, we continue to specialize in European marques (BMW, Mercedes-Benz, Volvo) while welcoming all makes and models. Our experienced team combines technical expertise with genuine care for every vehicle that enters our facility.
